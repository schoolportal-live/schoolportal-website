Features

Everything a school needs

Simple, powerful tools that make a real difference for schools, teachers, and families — without the complexity.

Instant Communication

Send announcements, alerts, and updates to every parent in seconds. No more missed letters or unanswered calls — just clear, direct messaging that gets through.

Progress at a Glance

Parents see attendance records, grades, and milestones in real time. Schools spend less time generating reports and more time on what matters — teaching.

Events & Scheduling

Manage school events, parent-teacher meetings, and term calendars from one place. Parents RSVP instantly. No more back-and-forth scheduling headaches.

Secure by Design

Built with data protection and safeguarding at the core. Role-based access, encrypted messaging, and full GDPR compliance — so schools can communicate with confidence.
